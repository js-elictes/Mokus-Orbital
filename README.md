## MISSION ARCHITECTURE
Humanity must become a multi-planetary species. The gating function for this is your inability to focus for 25 consecutive minutes. Mokus Orbital solves this by wrapping standard temporal-blocking protocols (Pomodoro) in high-fidelity launch telemetry and degenerate gambling mechanics.

The architecture is fundamentally zero-dependency. No React. No NPM bloat. Just raw DOM manipulation and SVG math calculated on the fly. The best part is no part. 

## THE DOPAMINE EXPLOIT (GAMBLING MECHANICS)
Standard productivity tools fail because they ignore the fundamental physics of the human brain: it craves variable rewards and existential risk. The feedback loop here is explicitly optimized to hijack these pathways.

1. **Temporal Staking (The Burn):** You set a burn duration. You initiate launch. If you break focus and scrub the mission mid-burn (FTS Activation), the vehicle is lost. More importantly, your entire accumulated net worth (Ð) is drained to zero. This simulates actual orbital stakes. You are risking capital every time you start a timer.
2. **Payload Deployment:** Successful orbit yields base Dogecoin (Ð).
3. **Variable Reward Lootboxes:** Unallocated capital is useless. You can stake earned Ð in the Terminal on 'Meme Data' (Tier I) or 'FSD Beta' (Tier II) lootboxes. The RNG uses heavily weighted multi-modal distributions (x1, x6.9, x42.0). You either extract massive ROI and a decoded transmission, or you burn capital for standard telemetry. It is literal slot-machine logic applied to task management.

## EXPANSION TREE
Base reality is Earth. You must scale operations by unlocking higher-tier gravity wells to maximize output.

* **Red Roadster (500 Ð):** Injects a permanent low-poly car into the orbital render loop.
* **Crew Dragon Rating (1,000 Ð):** Human-rated biological payloads.
* **Lunar Gateway (1,000 Ð):** Shifts environmental variables to lunar conditions. Applies a hard 5x multiplier to base Doge extraction.
* **Mars Base Alpha (5,000 Ð):** 10x multiplier. The ultimate hedge against Earth-based failure modes.
* **Boring Flamethrower (5,000 Ð):** Injects a persistent screen-blend fire overlay across the entire DOM because it is objectively funny.
